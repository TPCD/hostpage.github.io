I am now a second-year Ph.D. student under the supervision of under the supervision of Prof. Michael S. Lew, at the LIACS Media Lab group of Leiden University. Previously, I got Bachelor and Master degree from Soochow University and Southwest University in 2015 and 2018, respectively. During the Ph.D. study, I worked with Dr. Liu Yu.
