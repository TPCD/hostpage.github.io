About Me

I am now a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Prof. Nicu Sebe, at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG) of University of Trento. Previously, I got Ph.D. degree from Leiden University in 2022. During the Ph.D. study, I worked with Dr. Zhun Zhong and Dr. Yu Liu.
